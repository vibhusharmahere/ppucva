About Us

Welcome to our website, where we use cutting-edge technology to predict personality via CV analysis.

We know that personality traits are important factors in job performance and job satisfaction, which is why we use the Myers-Briggs personality test to help job seekers find positions that are a good fit for their personality.

The Myers-Briggs personality test is a widely-used tool for assessing personality. Based on the theories of Carl Jung, it identifies four pairs of personality traits:

The four pairs of traits are:

  • Extraversion vs. Introversion
  • Sensing vs. Intuition
  • Thinking vs. Feeling
  • Judging vs. Perceiving

By analyzing CVs using the Myers-Briggs personality test, we can help job seekers identify their strengths and weaknesses and find positions that are a good match for their personality. Our sophisticated algorithms use machine learning to analyze large amounts of data and make accurate predictions about job fit.
